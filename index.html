Our History

Crafted Treasures was founded in 2010 by Emily and James Carter, starting as a small online store offering their handcrafted jewelry and home decor. Their passion for handmade artistry and collaboration with other craftsmen led to a diverse product range and a loyal following.

In 2015, they opened their first brick-and-mortar shop in their hometown, quickly becoming a local favorite known for high-quality, unique pieces and a welcoming atmosphere. Each item reflects their commitment to sustainability, quality, and the beauty of handmade craftsmanship.

Today, Crafted Treasures thrives both online and offline, offering artisanal goods from around the world. Dedicated to supporting independent artists, the shop provides customers with unique, meaningful items, bringing joy and inspiration through the beauty of handmade treasures.
